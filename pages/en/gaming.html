Why do I play Games?



"I play for fun, always looking for new challenges!"

Unlike many people I know, my main goal is to play games for entertaining. When I get to a game which brings me no entertainment, no challenges or have challenges that gives me a "frustration feeling", nothing better than just switch to another!
I almost always fall in love with the lore in games and for that reason I decided to play and make videos/streams of my gameplays, focusing as much as possible in showing the history told by them on my channel.

"I don't consider myself toxic or elitist.
That's why I hate 100% those behaviors."

That's it! Although I have a few competitive games on my game library, I try my best to don't play them in the "competitive mood", considering that "the I AM THE BEST show off feeling" always kills all the fun of the gameplay! I have to say here, there are even certain games I surely would NEVER add to my gaming library!

Where do I play?



"My personal preference is to play on PC..."

The reason for that is really simple. It is possible to find good games for free! Friends have already tried to convince me about the solo fact that platforms as XBox and Playstation have exclusive games... "So what?" - that is my thought! Right now I have a PC built more than 6 years ago and I can still play almost everything currently launched by this date! Buy a XBox/Playstation today, and you will have to buy next generation (version/build) in the following year to have access to the "exclusive title"! That is how I see things - and that is the main reason I play on PC.

"... but I have a big history on gaming, using consoles. This history begins on 1989!"
