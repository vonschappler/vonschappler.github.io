Informative note for Set/2021

So much happened lately... now it's sharing time!

It's been almost four long months without any updates for these notes... Yes I know I always am promising constant updates, but I can never make good on the promise. I realized that it has been difficult to maintain a constant flow of updates, when you are an independent content producer and you have to do it yourself.
Because of that, I made an unofficial decision (until now) to not promise updates to the website, but do so when possible. There is a lot going on currently and these things have either been taking up my time or making me change my priorities.
I'm not going to say that I'm happy when I need to do these such things, not only because changes may trigger anxiety attacks, but also because I like to, at least, try to plan things in advance so I have a "Plan B", in case things get out of control. Unfortunately, since the pandemic came, it's not quite the that's been happening - and I've been caught off guard many times, of course, almost always with problems to solve and almost never with solutions to these (or other) problems.
As a result, I end up wearing myself out not only physically, but emotionally - something that worsens (a lot) my quality of life and reduces the little control I have over my mental health.

A brief moment for venting...


It's been about a year since I've chosen to assume publicly my condition (being autistic) as part of my life. There are many conceptual paradigms of what is or what is not autism, and many people are unaware of what it actually happens in the life of an adult diagnosed with autism.
You know, despite the fact that Brazilian's life can be described as "a life to get used to problems instead of solving it" (since most of the time, we solve a problem and only to get ten more later), it comes a time when individuals get tired of being judged by their behavior towards things, towards society, and acknowledge it is time to do something for themselves. With this in mind, I made this decision (expose publicity my mental health condition) hoping things will be different in the future.
I hope that someday these conceptual paradigms will be broken and that people will stop looking (not just at me, but at all other autistic people, regardless of their degree of autism) for who we really are. People with peculiar mental and social conditions, both in terms of cognitive learning and in terms of social behavior. I say this because I have my own choices "of things to do and how to do them" and most of the time I end up being judged for those choices. The difficulty in adapting to certain things suggested by others, makes me very stressed, and most of the time, this stress have made people go away from me. When they don't go away, they end staying close, but making judgments about things that are not part of my reality, but part of theirs, because they don't understand (or don't care about) what's going on inside my mind and/or in my life.
This is a very uncomfortable feeling and as much as I come to do something to ease this feeling, it seems that nothing has changed. Do you know that feeling of emptiness that sometimes some people can feel? Well, that's how I feel when these things happen to me. Needless to say, having people judging me and/or moving away from me, makes this emptiness feeling even worse.
As an autistic person, I have extreme difficulties in socializing - which is, by the way, a very common characteristic in every autistic person, regardless of the severity and/or level of autism, and "losing people" I still have as part of my social circle makes my social life even more complicated than it already is.
Apart from all that, there is the insistent search for recognition. Personal recognition and being recognized by others - mainly family and relatives. I believe I've already talked about it, but I'll repeat it. To most people, all I do is to pay pity over me only to "take advantage" of something. If my life depended on my relatives, it is possible that today I would be in a madhouse since I have tried several times to do things that "a normal person wouldn't do" - let's put it that way.
To be honest, I thought publicly acknowledging the fact that I'm autistic would change things for the better, personally and professionally. That's not what happened. That made things a lot worse, in my point of view, but I will not go back on that decision. There are those who don't like it when I say I am autistic. There are those who doubt that I'm autistic only because I don't look like one. And there is that small minority who doesn't care about that, understanding the situation and trying to help in whatever way he can. The fact is that each passing day, I look at the things that happened throughout that day and I realize that my fight (which wasn't easy) just gets even harder.
Despite the fact that "this autistic person here" has little empathy towards others, I've noticed that I have more empathy than many people out there, especially when I look to those that come for me only when they need my help. Well, they know I won't say no (as long as the help I can provide it's something within my reach), and that's because, unconsciously, helping is part of the "quest for recognition" and it ends in those people making profit of this.
Well, I won't go into detail about any of that here. I just used this little space as a venting moment, because, as I said, the emotional strain pushed over my is very high recently.

Without making an official publication on this website, I ended up making some changes regarding my streams. I usually make the announcement here and then start applying the changes, but as I said before, the lack of time forced me to reverse the order of this process a bit.

I believe that those who followed the change process realized I changed the puppet model that I use as my "facecam". Although it's something more difficult to modify it for specials streams like the ones I usually do on commemorative dates (such as Easter, Haloween, Christmas, among others) is a model with more life and movement - besides it looks far way better on screen than the previous one.

1.3. Acquisition and selection of streamed games:
After the review I did on this site about the game Outriders, I really thought that using my focus in this game would be a good thing.
Things didn't go the way I had planned. Let's say the lack of in-game content, support ineffective by the developers, added to the various bugs (some of them seem like they never were fixed and will never be - by the way, I have a feeling they never noticed my humble messages mentioning some persistent bugs in the game) took part of my discouragement and lack of interest to continue creating content for that game.
Selecting a game to stream is no simple task. I've said a few times that I don't want to bring to my streams games I don't enjoy playing. But I'm also trying to think a little bit differently on the overall meaning of what I've been playing. I'm looking for games that not only give me pleasure to play but games that incite my curiosity and - of course - bring pleasure to those who are watching streams.
I also have to mention here that I have a decent collection of games, which has been expanded whenever possible. The motivation to bring variety to this community, whenever possible, makes me look for some new games to bring to the streams.
Thanks to advertising campaigns, community member's gifts, and giveaways, this collection growing bigger. This means that every now and then, as I've been doing in the past few months, I promote an advertising campaign delivery stream.
I recognize that I have not only to deliver those campaigns but also have to finish some unfinished games as a compromise to this community, including "Anima: Gate of Memories", "State of Mind", (fulfill the promise to attain all achievements during streams of) "Code Vein", among others.
I don't know when I'll be resuming these projects, but I've been doing some sporadic "State of Mind" streams when I need to relax because this game is really relaxing. As for the others, I really need to organize better my time and plan something different to do with them, but at the moment, these games are out of my best interest.
