What's new?


We are slowly creating new things for the channel as a way to keep our community active. Among these we are implementing system of currency in the channel. So far, this system is under test, but can be accessed in the chat through the command !vscoins.
In the future, with these coins you can participate in activities such as:
  • Giveaways - events promoted to reward viewers;
  • Minigames - minigames as !heist and !duel are being implemented for your enjoyment during lives;
  • Bets - when promoted you can increase your vscoins for use in other activities;
  • Opportunities to join my game sessions, when applicable.
Much more is coming to improve our interactivity! To know what is already available in our list of commands click here.

About the YouTube Channel


I had scheduled the return to YouTube in September, with pre-recorded gameplays of Bayonetta e Doom (2016). These plans have unfortunately not been implemented, for several reasons that should not be stated here. In contrast, this channel will remain active, but its current function will be to store and publish the live sessions in full. The way these videos will be uploaded / published is still under review, but I will keep you posted when something new happens there.
So far, I'm developing a basic script that I will use in my videos, as well as working on creating elements that will be used specifically for pre-recorded videos when they are re-released.
The contents/format of the pre-recorded videos which will to be recorded are still being planned. If you have any suggestions, feel free to contact me at my e-mail so we can work together to make something that please everyone!
